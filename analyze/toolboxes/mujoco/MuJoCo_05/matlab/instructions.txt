Entry-point instructions:

1) Type 'set_path'.

2) 'mj' is the mex interface to the MuJoCo physics engine. 
	Type 'mj' for quick help.

3) Load a model with mj('load','filename'). For example try
   mj('load', which('cartpole.xml'))

4) Plot the model by typing 'mjplot'.

5) Hit 'spacebar' to simulate.
